Doclyn is built with privacy as a top priority. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our application.

1. Information We Collect

Doclyn does not collect or store your personal documents on external servers.

  • All data is encrypted
  • Stored locally on your device
  • Not accessible by us

2. Device Permissions

Doclyn may request access to:

  • Storage (to save and retrieve documents)
  • Biometric authentication (fingerprint/face unlock)
  • Camera (for document scanning)

These permissions are used only for app functionality.

3. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your master password
  • We do not upload your documents
  • We do not sell or share personal data
  • We do not track your vault content

4. Security

Your data is encrypted using strong encryption methods. If you forget your master password, your encrypted data cannot be recovered. This ensures maximum security.

5. Data Responsibility

Since data is stored locally, uninstalling the app or clearing app data may permanently delete your documents.

6. Children's Privacy

Doclyn is not intended for children under 13 years of age.
